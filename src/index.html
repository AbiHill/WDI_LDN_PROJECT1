Find'ems

How to play:

The aim of the game is to find all of the hidden items in the room.

You will be displayed an item in the top right hand corner. Look for the item in the room and click on it once you find it. If you wish to skip an item then just click on the top right hand item.

You only have 30 seconds to find the items so hurry.

There are 18 items to look for, find at least 16 to head to the next level.

Good Luck!
